About us

As part of the DAAD's funding programme "Ukraine digital: Ensuring academic success in times of crisis (2022)", the University of Leipzig supports refugee and refugee students as well as doctoral students from Ukraine with its project "Leipzig Chance for Science". On the one hand, the project includes the creation and provision of a digital teaching offer with statistical content in English and Ukrainian. The offer is aimed at students in Bachelor's and Master's degree programmes in education and social sciences. On the other hand, a digital PhD workshop is being planned and implemented to promote academic exchange, build mentor-mentee relationships and provide guidance in the publication of journal articles.
